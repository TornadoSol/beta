FAQ

What is TornadoSol ?

TornadoSol is a high-volume cryptocurrency mixing service tailored to Solana users and holders. By using our platform, you gain the ability to obscure the origin of your SOL tokens, ensuring anonymity on the blockchain. Our service is optimized for both small and large transactions, and in the unlikely event of Solana main-net downtime, we prioritize swift transaction rescheduling.

How do I use TornadoSol ?

1. Input your deposit address/addresses. 2. Set your desired input delay; the longer, the better for privacy. 3. Save your session token for reference. 4. Send your SOL tokens to the generated address (minimum 1 SOL). 5. Receive your freshly mixed SOL tokens in one or multiple wallets.

How much time does it take ?

Typically, the entire process is completed in less than 15 minutes. Acknowledgment of your deposit into the generated account may take up to 15 minutes. If your deposit is not acknowledged after this period, please contact our support team with your session ID for assistance.

How anonymous am I ?

TornadoSol ensures complete anonymity by adhering to strict privacy practices. We do not retain logs or user information. Any data stored on our servers is retained only for as long as necessary to complete the transaction, and everything is automatically deleted within 24 hours after the mixing operation.

How can I contact you ?

For any issues or inquiries, please reach out to us via email. Don't forget to include your session ID for prompt assistance and support. Your satisfaction and privacy are our top priorities.
